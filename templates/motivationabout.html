Motivation

The pandemic has wrecked havoc in so many parts of our lives, including but not limited to our mental healths. As the pandemic becomes the hottest topic in current news outlets, it becomes increasingly difficult to stay positive and hold out hope. I personally have tried stirring away from news altogether, but I found it difficult to do. I still wanted to know the COVID-19 status of Indonesia, India, Nepal, UK, where my family and friends live. I also still wanted to know the situation in the US where I live, as well as the world overall.

I come up with this website, Dev and Good News, so people can keep informed about the COVID-19 situation in the world overall and in each country individually, while also stay positive and hold out hope by looking at good news on COVID-19.

Cov n Good News, keeps you updated and helps you hold out hope :)
